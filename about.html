About

Designing the operating model for the AI era.

HumanCentric Labs was founded on a simple belief: technology alone does not transform organizations. People, work, and operating models do.

Our mission is to help organizations become Highly Adaptable Organizations where humans and AI work together to create lasting value.

Our thinking

Organizations were designed for the last century.

The Industrial Age gave us the job. It gave us the department. It gave us hierarchy, headcount, and the org chart.

For a century, that model worked. It optimized for efficiency, scale, and control.

The AI era requires something fundamentally different.

When AI can perform parts of every role, the job stops being the right way to organize work. When markets change faster than planning cycles, hierarchy becomes a bottleneck. When knowledge is everywhere, control becomes less valuable than adaptability.

HumanCentric Labs is developing the frameworks, methodologies, and operating models that help organizations navigate this transition.

Core belief

Companies don't struggle because they lack AI. They struggle because their organizations cannot continuously adapt.

Our observation

AI amplifies the operating model that already exists. If the model is broken, AI accelerates inefficiency. If the model is adaptive, AI compounds advantage.

Our position

The organization itself is the product. Technology is the enabler. Our work is about redesigning organizations so they continuously create value.

Our journey

A decade of building toward this moment.

2014

The Service Model is developed. The foundational idea that every act of work is a service, every service has a customer, and every customer defines value. At the time, this was about organizational design. It would later become the foundation for something much larger.

2018

HumanCentric Labs is established as an executive advisory practice focused on human-centric organizational design, research, and transformation. The emphasis is on helping organizations redesign work around people rather than around processes.

2022

The AI inflection point. As generative AI enters the enterprise, it becomes clear that the Service Model is not just about organizational design. It is the missing layer between AI capability and organizational value. The question shifts from "how do we improve work?" to "how do we redesign work for a world where humans and AI operate together?"

2025

Talent+AI™ is formalized as the new unit of work. The Service Model Management System (SMMS™) is published. Human Equivalence™ and ROTA™ complete the operating model. The Highly Adaptable Organization becomes the destination. The Work Frontier is named.

2025 and beyond

Microsoft describes Frontier Firms. HumanCentric Labs provides the operating model to help organizations become one. The convergence of AI capability and organizational architecture creates the Work Frontier, where the next generation of competitive advantage lives.

Our mission

To enable organizations to thrive at the Work Frontier by redesigning work around people, services, and AI.

Redesign work

Replace the job-based model with a service-based model where every act of work creates measurable value for a defined customer.

Integrate talent and AI

Build Talent+AI™ work units where human judgment and AI capability are designed to be inseparable, creating outcomes neither could achieve alone.

Enable continuous adaptation

Help organizations compound their learning after every customer interaction, every service delivery, every decision.

A different kind of practice

What HumanCentric Labs is not.

We are not an AI consultancy. We don't implement AI tools or build AI products.

We are not a systems integrator. We don't deploy technology on behalf of vendors.

We are not a management consultancy in the traditional sense. We don't produce strategy decks that sit on shelves.

What we are.

We are building the operating model for the AI era.

SMMS™, Talent+AI™, Human Equivalence™, and ROTA™ are the result of a decade of research and practice in organizational design. They are original intellectual property, not ideas borrowed from a business school or extracted from a vendor playbook.

Our work sits at the intersection of organizational architecture and AI capability. We help enterprise leaders redesign how their organizations work so that AI investment translates into measurable business value.

We work with CEOs, COOs, Chief Strategy Officers, and Chief Transformation Officers at organizations that are serious about becoming Highly Adaptable.
